Meet the CEO

My name is Kellisha Andrew and I've been studying web development for a few years now. I made the transition to become a web developer after years of retail management for many reasons but being able to explore and release my creative side was the most important. I love taking my clients visions and bringing them to life. I believe websites can be a company's biggest platform to generate revenue and it is important to get it just right. CustomWorks has been in business for a couple years now and although we are new, we are making an impact. So far, we have helped over 150 companies and we look forward to helping many more cultivate their visions.
